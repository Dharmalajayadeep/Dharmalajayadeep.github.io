AI GOAL:
Guide students from admission until visa filing.

1. University Admission Process:
Apply 1. to Universities:
Complete application forms for chosen French universities.
Submit all academic documents (transcripts, certificates, etc.).
Provide proof of English proficiency (IELTS) or a Medium of
Instruction (MOI) certificate.
2.Prepare for Admission Interview:
Schedule and attend an admission interview with the university.
Await the admission decision.
3.Receive Admit Letter:
Obtain the official admit letter from the university upon successful
interview completion.
2. Campus France Application:
1.Apply to Campus France:
Submit an application through the Campus France portal.
Pay the Campus France fee of INR 18,000.
2.Campus France Academic Interview:
Prepare for an academic interview conducted by Campus France.
Attend the interview (online or offline).
Receive an interview letter from Campus France.
3.Obtain NOC:
Await the NOC (No Objection Certificate) email from Campus France
if the interview is cleared.
3. Visa Application Process:
1.Book VFS Appointment:
Book an appointment with VFS Global to submit your visa application
documents.
