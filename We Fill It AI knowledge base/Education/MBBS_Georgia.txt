passing necessary examinations like the FMGE in India.

IELTS or TOEFL.

(6-years program)
Here’s a useful summary to promote Georgian MBBS/MD:
Degree: Doctor of Medicine (Physician)
Duration: 5 years of medical school + 1-year internship
Intake: Typically in February and September
Accreditation: Georgian medical universities are registered with the
Georgian National Educational Accreditation Centre
International Recognition: Recognized by organizations like the WHO,
WFME, ECFMG, FAIMER (Foundation for Advancement of International
Medical Education and Research), and MCI
Pathway Programs: Eligible for ECFMG certification pathways like
Pathway 2, 3, or 4
Indian Graduates: MBBS graduates from recognized Georgian universities
can practice medicine in India after passing the Foreign Medical Graduates
Examination (FMGE)
Study Pattern: 5 years of Academics + 1 year of Mandatory Internship :
First 2 years will be Pre-Medical Subjects (anatomy, physiology,
pharmacology, biochemistry, etc)
Next 3 years Clinical Subjects
Exam Pattern:
5 semesters of MCQs
6th Semester - Theory, Practicals, Exams and Assignments
Country-wise Licensure Exams (training provided in tracks opted by
students) :
USMLE (USA track, American graduate curriculum)
PLAB (UK track, British curriculum)
AMC (Australia track, Australia curriculum)
FMGE - India (mandatory to practice in India)
