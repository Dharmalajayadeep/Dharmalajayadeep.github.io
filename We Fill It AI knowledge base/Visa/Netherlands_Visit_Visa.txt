NETHERLANDS VISIT VISA
Schengen Visa
STEP 1: DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FROM APPLICANT
Applicants must ensure all required documents are ready BEFORE starting the online
application.
1.Passport (Mandatory)
Original passport with minimum 6 months validity
Minimum 2 blank visa pages (front & back)
2.Photograph
One recent colour photo
Size: 3.5cm by 4.5cm
White background
3.Financial Proof
Original 3-month bank statement:
Minimum balance: ₹ 2,50,000 - Per person
Balance must be maintained consistently for the last 3 months
Bank stamp & signature required
Fixed Deposits (FDs) can be shown as additional support
Income Tax Returns for last 2 years
4.Employment Details
If Employeed :
Employment Letter (mentioning designation & salary)
Leave Approval Letter (Mandatory – must mention travel dates)
Salary Slips (Last 3 Months)
If Self Employeed :
GST Certificate
Business Registration Certificate
Business Bank Statements (Last 3–6 Months)
Latest Income Tax Return
SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS
Cover Letter (explaining purpose, duration, who is paying)
Travel Itinerary
Flight Reservation (dummy booking – do not purchase tickets)
Travel Medical Insurance (minimum €30,000 coverage)
STEP 2: DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FROM HOST IN NETHERLANDS
1.INVITATION DOCUMENTS
Invitation Letter
Passport Copy (Host/Invitor)
Residence Permit Copy (if not Dutch Citizen)
Address Proof
Proof of Sponsorship and/or Private Accommodation Form (Official Dutch Municipality
Attested Form – recommended for stronger case)
STEP 3: VFS APPOINTMENT (Applicant Must Attend Personally)
1.At VFS:
Fingerprints
Digital Photograph
Document Submission
Fee Payment - (Visa Fee ₹9940 + VFS Service fee ₹1185) - ₹11795 Per person
PROCESSING TIME
Average Processing Time: 15 – 20 Calendar Days
Apply ideally 4–8 weeks before travel.
