CHINA VISA APPLICATION – STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE
Chinese Visa Application Service Centre – New Delhi
STEP 1: CHECK ELIGIBILITY & PREPARE REQUIRED DOCUMENTS (READ FIRST)
Applicants must ensure all required documents are ready BEFORE starting the online
application.
1.Passport (Mandatory)
Original passport with minimum 6 months validity
Minimum 2 blank visa pages (front & back)
Photocopy of:
Passport data page
Photo page (if separate)
Expired passports within the last 5 years (if any)
If applying for multiple-entry visa, passport validity must cover visa validity + duration of stay
2.Photograph
One recent colour photo
Size: 33mm × 48mm
White background
No headgear (unless religious)
Same photo must be uploaded online
3.Financial Proof (Mandatory – Very Important)
4.Employer certificate with income details OR
5.Original 6-month bank statement:
Minimum balance: ₹1,00,000
Balance must be maintained consistently for the last 6 months
Bank stamp & signature required
Temporary deposits will NOT be accepted
⚠️ If the bank balance condition is not met, the application will not be accepted
6.Invitation / Travel Documents
Provide either:
7.Invitation letter from:
Chinese travel agency OR
Individual in China (residence proof required)
OR
Round-trip air ticket booking
Hotel reservation
Tour itinerary
8. Covering Letter / Supporting Document (A printed and signed SOP must be submitted at
the Visa Centre along with other documents.)
Additional Documents (If Applicable)
Previous Chinese passports or visas
Proof of legal stay in India (for non-Indian applicants)
Name change certificate (if applicable)
Kinship certificate or property documents (if requested during online form)
STEP 2: ONLINE PAYMENT (VFS)
Visit: https://visa.vfsglobal.com/inn/en/chn/ff-info
Select service type:
Normal Service
Express Service
Complete online payment
Save the payment confirmation
STEP 3: CREATE ONLINE ACCOUNT
Open: https://bio.visaforchina.cn/DEL3_EN/qianzhengyewu
Click Create a New Account
Register using your email ID
Log in after account creation
STEP 4: FILL THE ONLINE VISA APPLICATION FORM
After login, click “Application Form” (top right)
Accept Terms & Conditions
Click your Email ID (top right)
Select Personal Centre
Click “Fill in a Form”
Select the Visa Centre
Click Confirm
A new website will open automatically: https://consular.mfa.gov.cn/VISA/node
Click “Start Filling in the Form”
Complete all details carefully
Upload your photo
Submit the application online
⚠️ The system accepts applications only for entry within the next 90 days
STEP 5: ONLINE APPROVAL & BARCODED DOCUMENT
Check Application Status
Log in to your account
Select the correct “Created Date”
Wrong date selection will show “No Data”
Barcoded Document / TE Official Invitation (Printed)
✔ If approval email is received:
Print:
Visa Application Certificate
Barcoded Document
✔ If approval email is NOT received but status shows
“Online Review Completed”:
Print:
Visa Application Form with barcode
Screenshot of the “Approved / Online Review Completed” page
You can still submit documents at the Visa Centre
STEP 6: SUBMIT DOCUMENTS AT VISA CENTRE (NO APPOINTMENT)
Chinese Visa Application Service Centre – New Delhi
Documents to Carry:
Visa Application Certificate / Barcode print
Original passport + photocopies
One physical photograph
Invitation / travel documents
Financial proof
Any additional documents requested
⚠️ Applicants for S, Q, X, Z & J visas must attend an embassy interview
STEP 7: PAYMENT AT VISA CENTRE
Service fees: Already paid online
Visa fees: Pay at Visa Centre
✔ Cash
✔ Demand Draft
STEP 8: SUBMISSION DEADLINE
Documents must be submitted within 15 days of online approval
Failure to submit within 15 days will make the application invalid
STEP 9: PROCESSING & COLLECTION TIME
Processing Time (From Submission Date)
Service Type Expected Collection
Express : 4th Working Day
Normal : 6th Working Day
Visa Centre Timings
Submission
Normal: 09:00 – 15:00
Express: 09:00 – 12:00
Collection
Express: 9:00–11:00 & 14:30–17:00 (4th Working Day)
Normal: 14:30–17:00 (6th Working Day)
Carry the Original Pick-Up Slip during collection
IMPORTANT DECLARATION
All information must be true and accurate
False or misleading information may result in:
Visa refusal
Denial of entry into China
Final decision rests with the Chinese Embassy / Consulate
