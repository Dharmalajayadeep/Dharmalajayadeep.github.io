- Australian visa decisions are made by immigration authorities.
- Financial capacity and travel intent are important.

To apply for an Australian tourist visa, you may need to provide the following
documents:
1. Passport Requirements:
Original Passport with at least six months validity from the date of travel.
Include copies of all passport pages (photo page and immigration stamps).
Attach copies of old passports (including used pages and visas) if available.
2. Application and Identification Documents:
Visa Application Form: Completed and signed form (Form 1419).
Family Form: Form M-54, duly filled and signed.
Proof of Identification and Residence:
Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, or Driving License.
3. Photograph Specifications:
Two recent passport-size photos (3.5 cm x 4.5 cm) with a matt or semi-matt
finish.
60%-80% face coverage, white background, and no borders.
Photo should not be more than 3 months old; no scanned or stapled
photos.
4. Covering Letter:
Covering Letter on business/official letterhead mentioning:
Name, Designation, Passport Number, Purpose, and Duration of Visit.
Addressed to “The Visa Officer, Australian High Commission, New
Delhi.”
Should be signed by an authorized signatory with company stamp.
Australian Visitor Visa Checklist
5. Invitation Letter (if applicable):
From the Australian host, with inviter’s passport copy and residence proof.
For tourism purposes, provide hotel bookings and a detailed day-wise tour
itinerary.
6. Marriage Certificate (if applicable):
If the spouse’s name is not endorsed in the passport and the visa is applied
post-marriage.
7. Financial Documents:
Personal Financials:
Bank Statements (last 6 months) with the bank’s name and contact
details clearly mentioned.
ITR for the last 3 years or Form 16 with PAN Card copy.
For Self-Employed Applicants:
Business ownership proof with financial papers, including 2 years of
ITR and 3 months of bank account statements.
For Employed Applicants:
Latest 3 months’ salary slips.
Salary bank account statements for the last 6 months.
Leave Sanction Letter or NOC from the employer.
8. Additional Documents (if applicable):
Children:
Birth certificate and School/College ID copies.
Travel and Tour Documents:
Air Tickets, Internal Tickets, Hotel Confirmations, Travel Insurance.
Covid Vaccination Certificate: Both doses required.
Health Insurance for 3 months (Mandatory)
9. Additional Financial Proofs (if required):
Bank statements showing sufficient funds (approx. INR 8 lakhs per head).
Short-term funds for travel (approx. INR 2.1 lakhs for 2 weeks).
10. Additional Supporting Documents:
Statutory declaration of financial support from a relative (if applicable).
Proof of change of name (e.g., Marriage Certificate).
Visa application fee receipt.
11. Processing Time:
Minimum 20-25 working days after submission to the Australian High
Commission.
12. Travel Confirmation Documents:
Confirmed return flight tickets.
Any other relevant travel confirmations.
